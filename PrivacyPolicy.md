# GB Playbook Privacy Policy

GB Playbook does not collect any data.  The publisher does not store any user data.

Third party distribution services (app stores) make some anonymous aggregate user information available to the publisher, such as the number of users in a particular country and the make and models of devices the app is installed on.  This data is not intended to identify individual users.  The publisher of GB Playbook will not share this app store data with anyone.
